Make a smart move! Get your mates on to digiSavings

Refer a friend to get on to digibank and upgrade to digiSavings and you get Rs. ₹250. More friends you get on board, more you can earn.

Validity

Only referrals and digiSavings openings from 15th July, 2016 to 31st December, 2016 will be gratified.

Usage limit: multiple use

Instructions

  • Offer is open to digibank e-wallet and digiSavings customers.

  • Transfer money (₹1 onwards) to your friend using the ‘Pay through Mobile’ option.

  • Your friend downloads the digibank app to receive the money.

  • Once your friend upgrades to digiSavings, we’ll add ₹250 to your digibank account (e-wallet or digiSavings)

Terms & Conditions

  • The referrer authorizes DBS Bank Ltd., it associates, subsidiaries and affiliates to contact him/her

  • Fixed incentive of ₹250 will be credited in your digi account (wallet or digiSavings) of the eligible referrer within 15 days of the Referee opening a digiSavings account

  • Referee should not be an existing digibank account holder

  • If two different referrers have referred the same referee, the one who refers first will get the fixed incentive
