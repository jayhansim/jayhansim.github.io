Encountering input field issues?

We have the solution for you. Simply follow the step below, and you should be on your way to opening your digibank e-wallet.

  • Re-enter email field is disabled even after filling in email address
  • OR
  • Button to go to the next page is disabled even when the form is complete.
  • Always use the tick button on your keyboard to go to the next question or page
  • Do not use keypad minimise button to hide your keyboard
  • Now you're able to fill in the form and proceed to the next page!
