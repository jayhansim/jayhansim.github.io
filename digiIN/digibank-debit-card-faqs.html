Get ready to enjoy quick and easy cashless payments, amazing offers on your favourite brands and up to 10% cashback with your digibank VISA debit card. Here's everything you need to know about your card.

How do I activate my debit card? Can I set my debit card PIN?

For security reasons, your debit card will be sent in a deactivated mode to your mailing address. To start using it, simply follow these steps:


Step 1. Select "activate your debit card" from the main screen or from the app menu
Step 2. Enter CVV (you'll find this at the back of your card)
Step 3. Set your 4-digit PIN
Step 4. Ta-daa! Your card activation is completed.

Where do I find my card details?

The card number and expiry date are printed on the front of the card. Locate your CVV on the back of the card. It's the 3-digit number to the right of your signature (and don't forget to sign your card!)

Can I use my debit card for online purchases?

With your Visa debit card, enjoy shopping at over 1,00,000 online merchants. To enable safe transactions for your security, only online transactions which are authenticated via an OTP password are permitted through digibank debit card. Your physical debit card also allows you to enjoy unlimited, free withdrawals at nearly 2 lakh ATMs in India. You can manage your card from the home screen in your digibank app or by accessing your card details in the app menu.

What are my debit card transaction limits?

To view your limits, please select "VISA Debit Card" on the home screen or from the app menu once your card has been activated.

What happens to my e-wallet virtual debit card?

Once you receive and activate your physical debit card, it will fully replace your virtual debit card. You can only have one card at a time.

How can I temporarily block my debit card?

You can block your card by accessing your debit card details on your home screen or through the app menu. Just flip the "Temporary Block" toggle to block your debit card for up to 60 days. Please note that your card will be permanently blocked if you don't unblock it after 60 days.

What do I do, If I lose my debit card?

You can block your card by accessing your debit card details on your home screen or through the app menu. Just flip the "Temporary Block" toggle to block your debit card for up to 60 days. Please note that your card will be permanently blocked if you don't unblock it after 60 days.

How can I enable my debit card for international use?

You can enable your card for international use by accessing your debit card details on your home screen or through the app menu. Just flip the "International Use" toggle to start living it up overseas. Please note that your debit card can be used internationally only at physical establishments and not at online websites.
