How to register

digibank Prerequisites:

  1. Get ready with your DBS ATM Card / Debit Card
  2. Ensure you have access to your phone number that is registered in your DBS Bank Account
  3. Remember your valid Email address for completing the registration
  • Download on the App Store
  • Get it on Google Play

digibank step-by-step registration:

  1. Choose to register a new account
  2. Enter your ATM/Debit Card number and PIN
  3. One-time password will be sent to your registered phone number. Enter the OTP in the screen
  4. Choose just 1 main transactional account for mobile banking
  5. Create username and password
  6. Congratulations! You’re registered to digibank account
